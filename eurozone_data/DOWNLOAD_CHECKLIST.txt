QUICK TIPS
================================================================================

For Eurostat downloads:
  1. On the dataset page, select your filters (geography, time, etc.)
  2. Click the "Download" icon (arrow pointing down)
  3. Choose "Full extraction" and "CSV" format
  4. Click "Download"

For Trading Economics:
  1. Scroll to the chart
  2. Click "Download" button on the right
  3. Select CSV
  4. May require free account (30 seconds to create)

For ECB SDW:
  1. Navigate to the dataset
  2. Use "Export" or "Download" option
  3. Select CSV format
  4. Choose time range (last 10 years is good)
